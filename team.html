How We Work Together

Collaboration and support are at the heart of how we operate every day.

💬

Daily Stand-Ups

Every morning the team has a short 10-minute check-in to share progress, flag any blockers, and plan the day together.

📊

Code Reviews

All code is reviewed by a more experienced developer before going live. This is one of the best ways to learn and improve quickly.

🎓

Learning Fridays

Every Friday afternoon is dedicated to learning. Developers explore new tools, watch tutorials, or work through online courses together.
