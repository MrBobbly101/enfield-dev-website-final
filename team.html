How We Work Together

Collaboration and support are important parts of how the team works each day.

💬

Daily Stand-Ups

Every morning the team has a short check-in to share progress, discuss blockers and plan the day.

📊

Code Reviews

Code is reviewed by a more experienced developer before it goes live. This helps trainees learn from feedback.

🎓

Learning Fridays

Friday afternoons are used for learning. Developers can explore new tools, watch tutorials or work through online courses.
