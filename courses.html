Recommended Courses

Whether you are just starting out or brushing up your skills, these resources will help you get ready for a role at Enfield Dev Ltd.

Free Online Resources

These platforms are completely free and excellent for building your skills from scratch.

💻

freeCodeCamp

One of the most popular free coding platforms in the world. Covers HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and much more. Earn certifications as you go.

Best for: Complete beginners who want structured learning with certificates.

Visit freeCodeCamp
⚙

The Odin Project

A free, project-based curriculum covering front-end and full-stack development. Highly respected in the developer community.

Best for: People who learn best by building real projects.

Visit The Odin Project
📘

MDN Web Docs

Mozilla's official reference for HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The most accurate and detailed documentation available online.

Best for: Looking things up, understanding how web technologies actually work.

Visit MDN
🎓

Codecademy

Interactive, browser-based coding lessons. Great for learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in a guided, step-by-step format.

Best for: Beginners who want immediate, hands-on practice with instant feedback.

Visit Codecademy
🌏

W3Schools

A long-standing web development reference and tutorial site. Simple explanations with live code examples you can edit instantly.

Best for: Quick lookups, simple tutorials, and testing snippets of code.

Visit W3Schools
🌱

Khan Academy

Free computing and programming courses aimed at beginners. Well explained with short video lessons and interactive exercises.

Best for: Younger learners or people who prefer video-based lessons.

Visit Khan Academy

Formal Qualifications

These qualifications are recognised by employers and may strengthen your application with us.

BTEC Level 3 National in IT

Pearson • Typically studied at sixth form or college

Recommended

The qualification many of our current trainees hold. Covers web development, programming, networking, and IT project management. An excellent foundation for a career in web development.

Web Developer Apprenticeship (Level 3)

Apprenticeship Standard • Earn while you learn

Earn & Learn

A government-backed apprenticeship standard covering front-end and back-end web development. Some of our staff joined via this route. Provides a recognised qualification while working in the industry.

HNC / HND in Computing

BTEC Higher / Pearson • College or university

Higher Level

A higher-level qualification that can lead directly to a Junior or Mid-Level role. Covers software development, databases, web technologies, and project management in depth.

Our In-House Training Programme

When you join us, your learning does not stop. Here is what we teach during your first three months.

📃

Weeks 1–2: Foundations

Setting up your development environment, understanding our code standards, learning how to use Git, and being introduced to our existing codebase and client projects.

📈

Weeks 3–6: Real Work

Working on live tasks with your mentor alongside you. Code reviews on every piece of work. Weekly one-to-ones to review progress and set goals.

🏆

Weeks 7–12: Independence

Taking on more responsibility, leading small features, and presenting your work to the team. By the end of month three, you should feel confident working independently.
