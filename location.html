Parking

Free on-site parking is available for up to 10 vehicles. Enter via the side gate on Hertford Road. Look for the Enfield Dev sign.

Getting Here

🚂

By Train

The nearest station is Enfield Lock on the Lea Valley line (operated by Greater Anglia). Trains run from Liverpool Street approximately every 30 minutes and take around 30 minutes.

From Enfield Lock station, it is a 10-minute walk south along Hertford Road. The office is on the right-hand side.

🚌

By Bus

Several buses serve Hertford Road directly:

  • 191 — Enfield to Walthamstow
  • 307 — Enfield Town to Brimsdown
  • 317 — Waltham Cross to Turnpike Lane

Alight at the Hertford Road / Riley Road stop.

🚗

By Car

From the M25: Exit at Junction 25 and follow the A10 northbound towards Enfield. Take the A1010 / Hertford Road exit.

From Central London: Take the A10 northbound from Tottenham. Continue through Edmonton and turn right onto Hertford Road at the Enfield border.

The office postcode for sat-nav is EN3 5JH.

🚲

By Bike

There is a bike rack at the front of the building. The area is well-served by the Lee Valley cycle path, which connects Enfield to Central London.

Nearby Landmarks

Useful points of reference if you are visiting us for the first time.

🏑

Lee Valley Park

A large regional park and nature reserve just minutes from our office, ideal for a lunch break walk. Covers 26 miles from Hertfordshire to East London.

🏝

Enfield Lock Station

Our nearest train station, approximately 10 minutes on foot. Regular services to Liverpool Street and Stansted Airport.

🏨

Enfield Town Centre

A 10-minute bus or car ride with a wide range of shops, restaurants, and the historic Enfield Market.
