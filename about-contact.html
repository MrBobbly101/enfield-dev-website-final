Our Story

Enfield Dev Ltd was founded in 2015 by two local developers, Rachel Okafor and Daniel Marsh, who wanted to build a web development agency that put people first — both clients and employees.

What started as a two-person operation out of a small office on Hertford Road has grown into a team of over 25 developers, designers, and project managers. We have delivered more than 300 websites and web applications for businesses across London and the South East.

We have always believed that the best way to grow a great company is to invest in great people. That is why we actively recruit trainees and juniors — and give them the support they need to thrive.

Our Values

These principles guide everything we do — from how we build websites to how we treat each other.

01

People First

We believe happy, supported developers write the best code. We make sure everyone on the team feels valued and heard.

02

Always Learning

Web development moves fast. We give every team member time and budget for training, courses, and conferences.

03

Honest Communication

We are straight with our clients and with each other. No jargon, no hiding problems — just clear, honest conversation.

04

Quality Over Speed

We would rather deliver a well-built website a week late than a buggy one on time. Good work takes the time it takes.
