Job Roles & Specifications

Full details on each available role — duties, required skills, qualifications and what we offer.

Entry Level

Trainee Web Developer

Enfield, London • Full-time • £22,000 – £25,000 per year

Apply Now

About the Role

This is an ideal first job for someone who has recently completed a web development course, bootcamp, or qualification such as a BTEC Level 3. You will be fully supported as you learn the ropes of professional web development.

You will work alongside experienced developers on real client projects, attend weekly learning sessions, and receive dedicated mentoring throughout your first six months.

Day-to-Day Duties

  • Writing and updating HTML and CSS for client websites
  • Fixing bugs reported by clients or the testing team
  • Using Git to manage and submit your code changes
  • Attending daily stand-up meetings with the team
  • Participating in code review sessions to learn best practices
  • Updating website content using a CMS such as WordPress
  • Writing internal documentation and commenting your code clearly

Required Qualifications

  • BTEC Level 3 in IT, Computing, or Web Development (or equivalent)
  • A portfolio of at least 2–3 personal or college projects
  • Right to work in the United Kingdom
  • Good written and spoken English communication skills

Technical Skills Required

  • Basic understanding of HTML5 structure and semantic elements
  • Ability to write CSS for layout, styling, and simple animations
  • Understanding of what JavaScript does (some practical experience is a bonus)
  • Awareness of responsive design and why it matters
  • Familiarity with browser developer tools

Soft Skills Required

  • Genuine enthusiasm for learning and improving
  • Ability to take feedback and apply it constructively
  • Good time management and ability to meet deadlines
  • Team player who communicates openly
  • Problem-solving mindset — willing to try before asking for help

What We Offer

  • Salary of £22,000 – £25,000 depending on experience
  • 28 days holiday including bank holidays
  • Dedicated mentor for your first 6 months
  • Annual training budget of £500
  • Hybrid working after probation (3 months in office)
  • Free on-site parking
  • Access to weekly learning sessions every Friday afternoon
Junior

Junior Front-End Developer

Enfield, London • Full-time • £26,000 – £30,000 per year

Apply Now

About the Role

As a Junior Front-End Developer, you will be responsible for building the visual side of our clients' websites. You should be comfortable with HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript — and keen to develop your skills further with an experienced team around you.

Day-to-Day Duties

  • Building responsive web pages from design mockups provided by our design team
  • Writing clean, well-commented, and accessible HTML and CSS
  • Adding interactive features using vanilla JavaScript
  • Testing websites across multiple browsers and screen sizes
  • Collaborating closely with back-end developers to integrate front-end work
  • Raising and resolving bugs found during testing
  • Reviewing junior colleagues' code and providing constructive feedback

Required Qualifications

  • BTEC Level 3 or above in a computing/IT/web-related subject, or equivalent work experience
  • A portfolio demonstrating front-end projects (personal or professional)
  • Right to work in the United Kingdom

Technical Skills Required

  • Solid understanding of HTML5 and CSS3 including Flexbox and Grid
  • Working knowledge of JavaScript (ES6+) including DOM manipulation
  • Experience using Git for version control
  • Understanding of responsive design and mobile-first development
  • Ability to translate design mockups into working HTML/CSS
  • Awareness of web accessibility (WCAG guidelines)

Desirable Skills

  • Experience with Sass or another CSS pre-processor
  • Familiarity with Figma or Adobe XD
  • Basic knowledge of WordPress theming

What We Offer

  • Salary of £26,000 – £30,000 depending on experience
  • 28 days holiday including bank holidays
  • Annual training budget of £750
  • Hybrid working from day one
  • Clear promotion pathway to Mid-Level within 18–24 months
Junior

Junior Full-Stack Developer

Enfield, London • Full-time • £28,000 – £33,000 per year

Apply Now

About the Role

Our Junior Full-Stack Developers work on both the front-end and the back-end of client projects. You will need some experience with a server-side language and a basic understanding of databases. This is perfect for someone who enjoys the full picture of how a website works.

Day-to-Day Duties

  • Building and maintaining complete web applications end to end
  • Writing server-side code in PHP, including working with WordPress
  • Writing and optimising MySQL database queries
  • Integrating third-party APIs (payment gateways, maps, CRMs)
  • Supporting senior developers on larger projects
  • Debugging issues across both the front-end and back-end

Required Qualifications

  • HND, foundation degree, or degree in Computing/Web Development (or equivalent experience)
  • A portfolio of full-stack or back-end projects
  • Right to work in the United Kingdom

Technical Skills Required

  • Competence in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Experience with a server-side language (PHP preferred; Python or Node.js accepted)
  • Basic knowledge of relational databases — writing SQL queries, understanding tables
  • Understanding of HTTP requests, APIs, and JSON
  • Experience with Git for version control

What We Offer

  • Salary of £28,000 – £33,000 depending on experience
  • 28 days holiday including bank holidays
  • Annual training budget of £1,000
  • Hybrid working from day one
  • Quarterly performance bonuses
